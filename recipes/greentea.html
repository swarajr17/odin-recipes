Green Tea

Description

Green tea is a light and refreshing beverage made from unoxidized tea leaves, known for its delicate flavor and numerous health benefits. It is rich in antioxidants and often enjoyed hot or cold as a soothing drink. With its mild taste, green tea can be consumed plain or lightly sweetened.

Ingredients

  • Green Tea Leaves or Green Tea Bag
  • Hot Water
  • Honey or Sugar for sweetness.
  • Lemon Slice
  • Ice Cubes

Steps

  1. Boil water and let it cool slightly (about 80°C/175°F).
  2. Steep green tea leaves or a tea bag in hot water for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Add honey, lemon, or ice as desired, then serve.
